
Loading modules
---------------

code:purge(mymodule).
    Purges mymodule.
code:load_file(mymodule).
code:load_abs("/home/me/mymodule"). % no ".beam"
    Loads mymodule.
l(mymodule), nl(mymodule)
    Loads mymodule to this or all nodes.
c("/home/me/mymodule"). % no ".beam"
    Compiles and loads mymodule.
m()
    Shows loaded modules.
